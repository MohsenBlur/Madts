# Area and Perimeter of a Square

This interactive demo lets you drag and resize a square on the canvas. Watch the numbers in the info panel update live as you explore:

- **Side** — the current side length in pixels
- **Area** — calculated as `side × side`
- **Perimeter** — calculated as `4 × side`
